Archivists Supporting Activists

In response to the ongoing police violence at protests in the wake of the Minneapolis police murder of George Floyd, Documenting the Now put out a call for archivists to help support activists documenting this violence. These archivist volunteers practice non-extractive, anti-racist ethical approaches to archiving, and have attested that they are in no way connected to law enforcement.

This list is meant to be used as a resource for activists who could use assistance in building their archives.
Sign up to volunteer your archival skills and services.
